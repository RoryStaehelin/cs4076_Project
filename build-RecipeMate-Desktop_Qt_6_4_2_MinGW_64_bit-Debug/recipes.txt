---Basic omelette recipe
Ingredients
3 eggs
1 tsp sunflower oil
1 tsp butter
Instructions
1. Season the beaten eggs
2. Heat the oil and butter
3. Pour the eggs into the pan
4. Fill the omelette with whatever you like

---Peanut Butter Cookies
Ingredients
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup white sugar
1 egg
Instructions
1. Gather all ingredients.
2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). 
3. Mix peanut butter
4. Roll mixture into 1-inch balls
5. Flatten each with a fork, making a criss-cross pattern. 
6. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are firm, about 10 minutes
7. Enjoy!
